Directions

The symposium will take place at Senaatszaal in the same building where the Aula is situated. 
The address is Comeniuslaan, 2, Nijmegen.

Nijmegen is easily reachable by train (for train schedules take a look here). 

From Nijmegen central station, there are several buses going to Comeniuslaan. 

 You can take bus 10 (destination Heyendaal) or bus 3 (destination Wijchen). 
Get off at busstop 'Bestuursgebouw'.

You can also take bus 6 (destination Brabantse Poort) or bus 9 (destination Grave). 
Get off at busstop 'Tandheelkunde'.

For more details on the locations of the bus stops check the map here. 

To help you plan your journey with public transport  (inside the Netherlands) you can always use the website 9292. 

If you come by car, you can park just next to the Aula (P6). Just follow Comeniuslaan and you will see the entrance to P6.
