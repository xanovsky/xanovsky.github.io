Activities

I have served on several program committees: ICFP 2017, ICALP 2017, MFPS 2017 (chair), PLDI 2017, FORTE 2017 (co-chair), ... (see more).

I am the chair of Conference Committee of ACM SIGLOG, as well as the Secretary of its Executive Committee. I am a member of the Steering Committe of LICS, CMCS and ICE.

I was the co-chair of the organization committee of CALCO 2015, MFPS 2015, and CMCS'10 and the publicity chair of CMCS and CALCO in the period 2012-2015. I was also a member of the organizing committee of DisCoTec'10.

I have given invited talks at TACL 2017,RAMiCS 2017,CWI Lectures in honour of Adriaan van Wijngaarden (100th anniversary), CSL 2016, BLC 2016, ICE 2016, WIP 2016, CMCS 2016 (invited tutorial speaker), CONCUR 2015, WOLLIC 2015 (invited talk and tutorial), TRENDS 2014, MFPS 2014, APLAS 2013, FICS 2013 and TbiLLC 2013.

From 2011-2015, I was the site representative in Nijmegen of COIN: Coalgebra in the Netherlands, a bi-monthly seminar, alternatingly taking place at CWI and the Radboud University Nijmegen. From 2012-2014, I co-organized the Brouwer seminar. From 2008-2011, I organized with Frank de Boer the ACG seminar.
