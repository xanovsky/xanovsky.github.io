I have given invited talks at MFPS 2014, APLAS 2013, FICS 2013 and TbiLLC 2013.

I am the site representative in Nijmegen of COIN: Coalgebra in the Netherlands, a recently started bi-monthly seminar, alternatingly taking place at CWI and the Radboud University Nijmegen. From 2012-2014, I co-organized the Brouwer seminar. From 2008-2011, I organized with Frank de Boer the ACG seminar.
