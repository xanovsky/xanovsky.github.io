Activities

I have served on several program committees: FORTE 2017 (co-chair), ESOP 2017, LSFA 2016, FORTE 2016, ICE 2016, CMCS 2016, LICS 2016, ... (see more).

I am the chair of Conference Committee of ACM SIGLOG, as well as the Secretary of its Executive Committee. I am a member of the Steering Committe of LICS and ICE.

I was the co-chair of the organization committee of CALCO 2015, MFPS 2015, and CMCS'10 and the publicity chair of CMCS and CALCO in the period 2012-2015. I was also a member of the organizing committee of DisCoTec'10.

I have given invited talks at CONCUR 2015, WOLLIC 2015 (invited talk and tutorial), TRENDS 2014, MFPS 2014, APLAS 2013, FICS 2013 and TbiLLC 2013.

From 2011-2015, I was the site representative in Nijmegen of COIN: Coalgebra in the Netherlands, a bi-monthly seminar, alternatingly taking place at CWI and the Radboud University Nijmegen. From 2012-2014, I co-organized the Brouwer seminar. From 2008-2011, I organized with Frank de Boer the ACG seminar.
