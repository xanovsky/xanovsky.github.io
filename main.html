Short Bio

I am a tenured assistant professor at the Institute for Computing and Information Sciences, Faculty of Science, Radboud University Nijmegen, The Netherlands.

This webpage gathers all the information about my teaching and research, including my publications and talks. You can read about me, my professional activities, check where I am at the moment and download my CV.

My main research focuses on the modular development of specification languages and algorithms for models of computations. A lot of my work is developed from the unifying perspective offered by coalgebra, a mathematical framework established in the last decades.
