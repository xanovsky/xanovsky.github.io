Alexandra Silva

A tenured assistant professor at the Institute for Computing and Information Sciences, Faculty of Science, Radboud University Nijmegen, The Netherlands.

Alexandra's main research focuses on the modular development of specification languages and algorithms for models of computations. Her work is rooted on a mathematical framework: coalgebra.
