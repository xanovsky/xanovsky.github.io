Teaching

The CS and the Math departements at the Radboud run a joint Master in Foundations of Theoretical Computer Science.

I have several projects for bachelor and master level, mostly in formal languages, logic, and functional programming. If you are interested in doing your bachelor/master thesis with me, send me an email and we can discuss concrete projects. You can also take a look here (look for Silva) for some project examples.
