Directions

The symposium will take place at the Radboud University Nijmegen:
  • 8th Sep: HG00.128, ground floor of the Huygens building.
  • 9th Sep: LIN5, first floor of the Linnaeus building.
See map here for the building locations.

Nijmegen is easily reachable by train (for train schedules take a look here). 

From Nijmegen central station, there are several buses going to the university. The most convenient one (it runs every 10 min) is bus number 10. Get off at bus stop 'Huygens gebouw'.

For more details on the locations of the bus stops check the map here. 

To help you plan your journey with public transport  (inside the Netherlands) you can always use the website 9292. 

If you come by car, you can park at the Faculty of Science, see here for location. Please let me know if you come by car so that I arrange for a free exit parking ticket.
